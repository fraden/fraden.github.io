Development of a model-based predictive control for Active Heave Compensation systems

grade: 1,3

  • Mathematical and physical modeling of an Active-Heave-Compensation System
  • Development of an Extended Kalman Filter for Estimating the unmeasurable States.
  • Development of a Model-Based Predictive Controller for Stabilization of Vertical Movements
  • Set up the optimization problem with the features state prediction, hard and soft constraints for states and changes of states.
  • Evaluation and comparison with the conventional system (PID control)
